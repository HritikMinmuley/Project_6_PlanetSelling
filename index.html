Mercury

Mercury is the smallest and innermost planet in the Solar System, orbiting closest to the Sun. It has a very thin atmosphere, resulting in extreme temperature fluctuations between day and night. It takes about 88 Earth days to complete one orbit around the Sun and about 59 Earth days to rotate once on its axis.
